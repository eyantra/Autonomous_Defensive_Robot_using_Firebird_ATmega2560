Function that actually receives the signals(commands) from the computer over the zigbee communication channel. It takes in the degree values and rotates the servo motors on the bot accordingly. 

the degree sent from the computer is half the actual angle of rotation so that it can be sent in one byte. Hence, the actual degree is 2 times the received degree value.
